Kronk's Spinach Puffs

Kronk, the beloved character from 'The Emperor's New Groove and his own spin off film, 'Kronk's New Groove' was shown to be a fantastic chef, with one of his signature dishes being the spinach puff! Taking inspiration from this, we have devised our own recipe for the tasty treat!

Ingredients (Serves 4-6)

Approximately 300g of thawed spinach
1/2 cup of crumbled feta cheese
1/4 cup of finely chopped onion
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 teaspoon chopped dill (or an alternative herb such as basil)
1 teaspoon minced garlic (or garlic powder)
Salt and black pepper
2 large eggs
1 sheet frozen puff pastry, thawed and rolled out to a 12-inch square and kept chilled

Method

  • Squeeze out spinach until dry, mixing with the next 5 Ingredients
  • Season with salt and pepper
  • Beat 1 egg to blend and fold into the spinach mixture
  • Cut the pastry into 3 equal strips, keeping one strip for another use
  • Cut each strip into 3 again, totalling 6. Place a square in each cup of the muffin pan, pressing the bottom and sides down, leaving the corners pointing up
  • Divide the filling between cups and fold the pastry corners over the filling, pressing the corners to meet in the centre, cover the puffs and chill
  • Preheat the oven to 400 degrees, beating the remaining egg to blend in a small bowl
  • Brush pastry with the egg, giving it a nice sheen and bake for around 25 minutes, or until the pastry is golden brown and puffy
  • Transfer to a wired rack and let the puffs cool for around 10 minutes in the pan
  • Run a sharp knife around the pan edges to loosen and turn the puffs out onto the rack to cool before serving

    • Nutritional info

    These spinach puffs are a healthy treat, with only 130 calories! Spinach is high in protein, fiber and vitamins, giving your body all it needs to overthrow the evil Yzma!

    Alternatives
    Extra ingredients can be added for a variety, inlcuding bacon, chorizo or other goodies!
