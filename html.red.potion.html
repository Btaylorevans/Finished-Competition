Red Potion

The red potion is a staple in traditional RPG gaming, appearing in such titles as The Legend of Zelda and Minecraft. It usually acts to replenish the health of a character!

Ingredients (serves 2-3)

3 tablespoons of frozen cherryade
4 tablespoons of strawberry jelly
235ml of soda water
(optional) red food colouring

Method

  • Blend together the jelly and frozen cherryade
  • Pour the newly made mixture into a serving cup
  • Add the soda water and stir until the mixture is of desired consistency and taste
  • Pour into a suitable bottle and enjoy!

    • Nutritional info

    This recipe is a mix of differing fruits known for their vitamin content and antioxident properties. Ginger is known to ease muscle ache and act as an anti-inflammatory, creating a potion that will certainly replenish your health!

    Alternatives
    To create a green potion, use green jelly and limeade, or switch out food colouring!
